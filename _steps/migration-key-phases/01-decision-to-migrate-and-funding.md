The practice and Clinical Commissioning Group (CCG) assess the business case for the practice to migrate to a new clinical System. If agreed, the funding is secured by the Clinical Commissioning Group (CCG). Note: there may be [further costs identified by the Technical Survey](/prm-practice-migration/guide/technical-survey) for replacing hardware
